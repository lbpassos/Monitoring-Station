Monitoring-Station
==================

. Monitors Power Consumption, Relative Humidity and Temperature

Features:
---------
. Embebbed System implemented in ARM7 (LPC2106)

. SNMPv1

. ASIC (ADE7753)

. Temperature and Humidity Sensor (HoneyWell HIH6130)

. Display (Nokia 6100)

. Icinga as a remote monitoring station
